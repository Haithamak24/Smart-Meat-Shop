Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas? +

We currently deliver to all major cities within a 50-mile radius. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM. For deliveries outside our standard area, please contact us for special arrangements.

How do you ensure meat freshness during delivery? +

All our deliveries use refrigerated transport and insulated packaging with ice packs. We monitor temperature throughout the delivery process to ensure your meat arrives fresh and safe.

Do you offer bulk orders for restaurants? +

Yes! We have special pricing for bulk orders and commercial clients. Please contact our sales team at sales@smartmeat.com for a customized quote and delivery schedule.

What is your return policy? +

We stand behind our quality 100%. If you're not satisfied with your order, contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement. Customer satisfaction is our priority.
